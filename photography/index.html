First batch of HP5+ on Olympus OM1

I order myself an OM1 from eBay and jump headfirst into a new hobby. I'd always been a digital photographer and had shot here film a few times over the years but this time I was going to learn to develop rolls myself. To me, that's the only way it makes to be shooting on film, if I develop them myself. A future plan is to learn how to darkroom print at home as well.
