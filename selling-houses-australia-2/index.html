Selling Houses Australia

Modelled, textured and lit a CGI house for a Selling Houses Australia promo on Lifestyle channel. The house needed to look like it had a face and would need to show basic emotions. I also needed to design the house in three different states of disrepair for the story of the promo to make sense.

We went through several iterations of the house to find the one that looked very obviously like a face. One of the iteration of the houses got quite close to looking almost like a derelict haunted house which never made the cut but nevertheless is my favourite.

Awards:
  • Promax ANZ 2020 Best Entertainment Promo - Gold

Creative Director: Mikki Katz
Promo Producer and Director: Stephanie Reinboth
3D Artist: Parag Satyal
