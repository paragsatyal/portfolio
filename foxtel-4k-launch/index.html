Foxtel 4K Launch

Launch ident for Foxtel 4K, Australia’s first linear TV channel broadcasting in UltraHD resolution. This was the first time I had to render frames in 4K resolution, and on top of that it needed to be in 50 frames per second. It was 8 times the number of pixels rendered compared that 1080p at 25 frames per second. Thankfully we had enough budget to purchase two new PCs with 3xTitanXp. Also my first job using Redshift renderer.

Project Manager/Creative Producer: Stefanie Reinboth
