Saglo Samaaj Opener

Saglo Samaj (सग्लो समाज) is a Nepali current-affairs TV program which focuses on solution-oriented coverage of issues of national importance. The central idea of the opener was to use diversity in fabric and topography to represent the diversity in voices and issues.

Directors: Eelum Dixit, Saania Shah
Art Director and 3D Artist: Parag Satyal
Original music composition: Rohit Shakya
